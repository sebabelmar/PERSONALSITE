Seba Belmar

Let’s build beautiful and meaningful things

web developer

I believe that technology is the most peaceful way to reshape the economy towards a more sustainable model. After a few years as a financial analyst and sustainability climate change consultant for EY Chile, I became dissatisfied with the pace of change in the large corporations we served.

Since 2012, I’ve being finding ways to contribute to the evolution and responsible expansion of an economy that thinks globally but acts locally. I got a hands-on education in sustainable development by consulting for a renewable energy and green construction company. At the same time, I decided to become a web developer. I knew I needed to move beyond MS Office skills to start building better solutions.

I want to bring my passion for telling stories through the lens of my camera, coding, teaching, learning and designing solutions that improve people’s lives to a work environment full of happy people. I leave my home with a smile and I always expect a bigger one on my way back.
